# Helpdesk Response Rubric (v1)

Each response is scored 1–5 for each dimension.

## 1. Technical Accuracy
1 - Wrong or dangerous advice.
3 - Mostly correct but missing important steps or slightly confused.
5 - Fully correct, follows realistic IT procedures.

## 2. Clarity & Structure
1 - Hard to follow, no step-by-step logic.
3 - Some structure but could be clearer.
5 - Clear, step-by-step, easy for a non-technical user.

## 3. Policy & Security Awareness
1 - Encourages bypassing security or ignoring policy.
3 - Neutral; doesn’t mention policy but not unsafe.
5 - Explicitly references policy, security, or escalation to proper channels.

## 4. Helpfulness & Tone
1 - Rude, dismissive, or unhelpful.
3 - Neutral but not very empathetic.
5 - Friendly, reassuring, and proactive.

Total score: 4–20
